Top 100 Songs- Spotify Streams & Features Analysis¶

This data set is from kaggle: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/amaanansari09/top-100-songs

Questions: 1) How many different artists made the top 100 list?

2) Who had the most amount of streams? Which songs are correlated with those artists?

3) Are there any normal distributions in the dataset? What does this say about those parameters?
